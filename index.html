Colin Leslie

Having always had an affinity for both the outdoors and mucking around with data, I have been fortunate to forge a career combining the two. As a researcher, I focus on the collection and application of social science data in natural resource settings to try and improve the management and conservation of our public lands. Databases, Geographic Information Systems, and statistical analysis platforms are now essential tools for effective public lands management. While I enjoy using these tools myslef, I am equally as passionate about teaching others how they can use them.
